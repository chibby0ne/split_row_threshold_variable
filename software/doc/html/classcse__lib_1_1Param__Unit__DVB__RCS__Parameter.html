Param<unsigned int> cse_lib::Param_Unit_DVB_RCS_Parameter::TCT_inner_code_ordering

This bit specifies the order of transmission of the encoded block for Turbo Code.

A "0" means the natural order, a "1" the reverse order (see clause 6.4.4.4).

In the natural order, all couples (A, B) are transmitted first, followed by all couples (Y1, Y2) that remain after puncturing and then all couples (W1, W2) that remain after puncturing.

In the reverse order, the couples (Y1, Y2) are transmitted first, in their natural order, followed by the couples (W1, W2), if any, and then finally by the couples (A, B).
